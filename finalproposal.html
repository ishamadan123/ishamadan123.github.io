Staying Clean! Final Project Proposal


Project Concept/Motivation:
“I want to build an hand sanitizer / hand soap dispenser based on the detection of hand movement. Placing this in my room will encourage me to keep my hands clean. I also want a display of how much is left to know when to switch the bottle out ”

Anticipated Bill of Materials:
Arduino Uno
9V Battery (for power supply)
Ultrasonic Sensor (for hand motion)
Servo Motor (for press mechanic)
Assorted resistors (for current-limiting)
LCD Display (quantity left)
3D printed (physical prototype)
